about us

what makes our tea special?

The original Nana mint has a clear, pungent and light aroma, and is a mint traditionally used in Moroccan mint tea. Hybrids and other mint varieties, including Yerba Buena, are sometimes used as an alternative to Nana mint. In Morocco, mint tea is sometimes flavoured with herbs, flowers or orange flower water. During the cold season, they add many hot herbs such as European mint and Avicenna wormwood.
